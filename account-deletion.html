Account Deletion Request

App: General

How to Request Deletion

If you want to delete your account, please use the "Delete Account" flow in the app's settings/account section. If the app does not include this flow, you can send your request through the developer contact channel.

Required Information

Your request may need to include the app name, your registered email address if any, and information required for identity verification.

Result

When the deletion request is approved, the relevant data is removed irreversibly. Certain data may be retained for a limited time due to legal obligations.
