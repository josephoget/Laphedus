Privacy Policy

App: General

Data Collection

Our apps process only the minimum data needed for the service to work. For Dynamic Pill, settings, capsule preferences, and selected system events are mainly processed on the device. Limited flows such as premium purchases may use the relevant payment and subscription infrastructure.

Data Use

Collected data is used to maintain the app experience, verify premium status, and protect security. Data is not sold for advertising or used to build advertising profiles.

Third Parties

Depending on the app, third-party providers may be used. For Dynamic Pill, subscription infrastructure may access limited technical data during premium purchase and entitlement verification flows. Data sharing with ad networks is not intended.

Contact

For any privacy or data deletion request, you can contact us at laphedusapp@gmail.com.
